My first research was computational: finding the lowest-energy structures of
small clusters, and understanding why they settle into the shapes they do.

I coupled **stochastic global search** (particle-swarm optimisation) to
**density-functional theory** to explore the energy landscape of carbon
nanoclusters and small atmospheric clusters — the search proposes candidate
geometries, DFT scores them, and the survivors map out the basins that matter.
