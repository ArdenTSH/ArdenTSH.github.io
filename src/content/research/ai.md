A parallel track runs through all of my work: building models that respect the
structure of the problem, and thinking carefully about what happens as these
systems grow more capable.

On the modelling side I'm drawn to **symmetry-preserving architectures** and
**interpretability** — models whose internals we can actually reason about, not
just measure. Alongside the technical work I write on **AI safety**.
